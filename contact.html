Growing up as the son of a construction worker, I was always in contact with the building industry. However, I soon became more and more interested in methods of construction and started to think more theoretically about our built environment. Later, the university had a great impact on my personal aspirations. Today, my work is centred around design based on a human scale, sustainability and environmental tolerability.
I enjoy working in a group, and I strongly believe that the best ideas are an outcome of collective discussions and processes. I would describe myself as a friendly and open person who is reliable and able to adapt reviews quickly.
